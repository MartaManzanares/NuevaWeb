academic program degree: double degree in computer engineering with business analytics

My subjects

The double degree in business analytics and computer engineering (bilingual) offered at the Francisco de Vitoria University consists of the education of both areas. business analytics teaches how to analyze information and data from different institutions both public and private, such as financial or marketing in this business aspect. While computer engineering provides competencies about the branch of engineering of computer science. The degree is 4 years in both careers, while the double There are 5, dedicating this last year solely to computer engineering. In the first semester of the 1st year (in which I am studying), we have 8 subjects. Those of computer engineering are Mathematics for Engineering 1, Introduction to programming and Fundamentals of Computer Engineering, which we will talk about later. And in the part of Business Analytics we have, Introduction to Big Data and data management, History of science and technology, Organization and Administration of companies 1 and Microeconomics. Some of them in English as it is a bilingual degree. Finally This is the subject of Personal Skills and Competencies, which is mandatory for all the races.
