Who I am?

My name is Marta López-Manzanares Pérez and I live in Las Rozas de Madrid. At 18 years of age I have known a large part of Spain thanks to various trips with my family and friends that I have known in various ways, such as a trip to Ireland or my town, Bejar in Salamanca. Thanks to the town I have been able to meet many people from all areas of Spain but above all from Salamanca city, and that is why I have been traveling since I met them at least 3 times a year to Salamanca. Salamanca is a city that I always remember with affection and from which I have been able know many corners of what is for me a place to disconnect from the fast life of Madrid, that is why I decided to create this website, so that you could learn a little more about this place
